How to pack your ShopAm order:

1. Pack the item properly in a box or mailer bag with a label, ensuring nothing is missing.

2. Clearly write the buyer's name, tracking ID,and the ShopAm hub's address and phone number on the label you'll attach to the package.

If you're in Lagos:

1. Go to your ShopAm orders and click on this specific order to view the details.

2. Scroll down to Delivery Status and choose either:

• Drop off at a ShopAm hub near you, or

• Request pickup

3. If requesting pickup, fill out the pickup request form and a rider will be assigned immediately.

If you're outside Lagos:

1. Go to your ShopAm orders and click on this specific order to view the details.

2. Scroll down to Delivery Status and select Drop off at a ShopAm hub near you.

3. Send the item to any ShopAm hub closest to you.
